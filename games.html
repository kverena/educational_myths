Educational Myths – Evidence & Research Methodology
Universität Passau

Four interactive activities to help you understand evidence quality, source types, and research methodology – the foundations of a strong deconstruction chapter.

🎯
Myth or Fact?
Eight statements about research and evidence. Tap Myth or Fact and find out how well you already understand how academic evidence works.
⏱ ~5 min Good for: Before the search
→
📂
Sort the Sources
Drag eight source descriptions into the correct category: meta-analysis, empirical study, theoretical work, or grey literature.
⏱ ~8 min Good for: After the search
→
⚖️
Evidence Strength Rater
Read four source abstracts and rate the strength of their evidence on a 4-point scale. Compare your rating with the expert assessment and explanation.
⏱ ~10 min Good for: After the search
→
🧠
Research Methods Quiz
Ten multiple-choice questions on effect sizes, research designs, database search strategy, and what evidence actually means. Questions are shuffled each time.
⏱ ~10 min Good for: Consolidation
