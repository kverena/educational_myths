Block 1 · approx. 25 min

Where do I stand?

Self-assessment against the assessment criteria
Rate each statement honestly based on your current progress in your seminar paper or presentation. There are no right or wrong answers; this is for your own orientation.
Scale: Not yet Beginning Developing Confident
Criterion 1Structure & problem statement
My myth is clearly and precisely formulated as a researchable question.
My paper / presentation follows the three-part structure: identification → deconstruction → conclusion.
My introduction and conclusion are aligned around the same central question.
Criterion 2Theory & sources
I have identified the key concepts and theories relevant to my myth.
I am synthesising my sources, not just listing or summarising them.
My sources are current, academically rigorous, and sufficient in breadth.
Criterion 3Methodology & argumentation
My argumentation engages with counter-evidence, not just confirming evidence.
My myth is scoped precisely enough to be supported by the evidence I have found.
I draw independent, well-reasoned conclusions from the literature rather than reproducing it.
Criterion 4Formal & linguistic presentation
My citation practice is consistent and complete.
My academic writing / presentation style is clear, precise, and objective.
I have documented my use of AI tools according to the seminar guidelines.

Block 2b · approx. 30 min

The step to conclusion — What does this mean for my myth?

Now — and only now — move from analysis to evaluation. Based on what you have found in your deconstruction: what does the evidence allow you to conclude about your myth? Complete the sentence starters below in as much detail as you need.
My myth states that… The evidence I have found suggests that…
The picture is more nuanced because…
One aspect I cannot yet answer with confidence is…
For educational practice, this means…
Block 3 · approx. 20 min

Conclusion outline — Sketching the final chapter

A strong conclusion does four things: it revisits the myth question from the introduction, synthesises the deconstruction's key findings without repeating detail, provides a clear answer to the myth, and reflects on implications. Sketch each element in bullet points.
